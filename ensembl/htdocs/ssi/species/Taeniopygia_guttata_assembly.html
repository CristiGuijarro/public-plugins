The bTaeGut1_v1.p assembly was submitted by Vertebrate Genomes Project on May 2019. The assembly is on chromosome level, consisting of 444 contigs assembled into 135 scaffolds. From these sequences, 33 chromosomes have been built. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 11,998,827 while the scaffold N50 is 70,430,603.
