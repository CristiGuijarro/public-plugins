The ASM240643v1 assembly was submitted by BGI-shenzhen on October 2017. The assembly is on scaffold level, consisting of 502,654 contigs assembled into 360,804 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 34,849 while the scaffold N50 is 1,761,345.
