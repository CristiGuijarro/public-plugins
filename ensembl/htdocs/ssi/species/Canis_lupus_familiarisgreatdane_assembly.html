The UMICH_Zoey_3.1 assembly was submitted by University of Michigan on May 2019. The assembly is on chromosome level, consisting of 1,793 contigs assembled into 794 scaffolds. From these sequences, 39 chromosomes have been built. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 4,722,218 while the scaffold N50 is 64,204,256.
