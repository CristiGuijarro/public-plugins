The AIIM_Pcri_1.0 assembly was submitted by Department Of Biochemistry, All India Institute Of Medical Sciences(aiims), New Delhi, India on May 2019. The assembly is on scaffold level, consisting of 184,239 contigs assembled into 179,332 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 103,131 while the scaffold N50 is 190,304.
