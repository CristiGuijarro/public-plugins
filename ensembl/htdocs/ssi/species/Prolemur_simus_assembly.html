The Prosim_1.0 assembly was submitted by Henry Doorly Zoo and Aquarium on 20 June 2018. The assembly is on scaffold level, consisting of 208,899 contigs assembled into 128,596 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 47,757 while the scaffold N50 is 2,710,671.
