The Eluc_v4 assembly was submitted by University of Victoria on April 2019. The assembly is on chromosome level, consisting of 1,395 contigs assembled into 811 scaffolds. From these sequences, 25 chromosomes have been built. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 3,396,779 while the scaffold N50 is 37,550,661.
