The UOA_Brahman_1 assembly was submitted by University of Adelaide on November 2018. The assembly is on chromosome level, consisting of 1,552 contigs assembled into 1,250 scaffolds. From these sequences, 30 chromosomes have been built. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 26,764,281 while the scaffold N50 is 104,466,507.
