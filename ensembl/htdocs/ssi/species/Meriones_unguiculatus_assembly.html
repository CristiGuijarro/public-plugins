The MunDraft-v1.0 assembly was submitted by Lucigen Corp. on June 2017. The assembly is on scaffold level, consisting of 143,857 contigs assembled into 68,793 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 46,546 while the scaffold N50 is 374,687.
