The ASM171598v2 assembly was submitted by Smithsonian Institution National Museum of Natural History on May 2018. The assembly is on scaffold level, consisting of 29,371 contigs assembled into 15,315 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 194,737 while the scaffold N50 is 16,643,792.
