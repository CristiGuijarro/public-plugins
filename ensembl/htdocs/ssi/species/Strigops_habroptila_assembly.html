The bStrHab1_v1.p assembly was submitted by Vertebrate Genomes Project on January 2019. The assembly is on chromosome level, consisting of 455 contigs assembled into 99 scaffolds. From these sequences, 26 chromosomes have been built. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 9,454,100 while the scaffold N50 is 83,147,210.
