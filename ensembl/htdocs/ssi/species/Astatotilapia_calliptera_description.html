Astatotilapia calliptera, the Eastern happy or Eastern river bream, is a species of haplochromine cichlid from southern Africa. Many species have been moved between the genera Astatotilapia and Haplochromis, and while some consensus has been reached in recent years, their mutual delimitation is still far from settled. Based on mtDNA, Astatotilapia as currently defined is polyphyletic.
